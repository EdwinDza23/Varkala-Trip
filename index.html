Day-by-Day Plan

Relaxed, scenic and experience-first. Not a temple run — a proper youth road trip.

Day 1 Kozhikode → Thrissur May 1 · Thursday
⚠You've just arrived from an overnight drive. Take a short rest before exploring. Don't rush — morning at Kozhikode beach is worth the slow start.
7:30 AM
Kozhikode Beach — Morning Walk
Fresh sea air, golden light. Walk the promenade. Try puttu + kadala or appam from a nearby stall. Low crowd, chill vibe. Group photos on wet sand.
📷 Photo Stop🍳 Breakfast
9:00 AM
SM Street (Mithai Theruvu)
Kerala's most iconic food street. Try Kozhikodan halwa (the real stuff), banana chips, biryani, and sweet shops. Buy packed halwa as gift for family — ₹80–120 box. Budget ₹100–150/person here.
🍡 Street FoodMust Stop
11:00 AM
Kappad Beach
18 km from city. Historic beach where Vasco da Gama first landed in India (1498). Low-crowd, rocky, scenic. A memorial stone marks the spot. Great for creative photos. Less touristy than city beach.
📷 Photo Stop🌊 Scenic
1:00 PM
Beypore Port + Lunch
Ancient port with massive wooden dhow boats (urus) hand-built by local craftsmen. Walk the port, see the boats up close. Eat at a local fish restaurant nearby — fresh catch, Kerala style. ₹150–200/person.
🐟 Seafood Lunch⚓ Heritage Port
3:00 PM
Drive to Thrissur via NH 66
~90 km. Coastal highway — pleasant and fast. 2 hours with one chai stop around Guruvayur. Put on the trip playlist, windows down, cruise vibes.
🛣 Coastal Highway~2 hrs
5:30 PM
Thrissur Round — Evening Vibe
The famous circular road around Vadakkunnathan Temple. Walk the round, grab chai, explore local cafes and street food. Try Kerala parotta + beef curry at any local joint (₹80–120). Evening crowd is great here.
🍽 Dinner🌒 Night Vibe
9:00 PM
Check In — Thrissur Stay
Budget lodge near Thrissur Round. ₹1,500–2,000 total for 10 people sharing rooms. Sleep early — tomorrow is the biggest and most scenic day. 5:30 AM departure needed.
🏠 Stay Night
Day 2 Thrissur → Munnar → Kollam → Varkala May 2 · Friday
⭐The most cinematic day — ghats, tea estates, backwaters, and coastal roads all in one. Early 5:30 AM start is non-negotiable to make the most of Munnar and catch Kappil at sunset.
5:30 AM
Depart Thrissur for Munnar
~120 km via Chalakudy → Neriamangalam → Munnar Ghat Road. 3–3.5 hrs. You'll enter mist and tea estate country by 7:30 AM — pure magic. Have quick tea from car flask before leaving.
⛰ Ghat Drive~3.5 hrs
8:30 AM
Breakfast in the Tea Estates
Pull over at a roadside stall surrounded by tea carpets. Kerala tea + pazhampori (banana fritters) + boiled eggs. The golden light through the mist at this hour is something else.
☕ Breakfast📷 Golden Hour
9:30 AM
Gap Road + Photo Point — Munnar Hairpins
The iconic stretch. Layer after layer of green tea carpets sweeping down hairpin bends. Park the cars, walk up the road. Take unlimited time here — this is the postcard shot of the trip.
📷 Iconic Shot🍀 Tea Estates
10:30 AM
Mattupetty Dam + Echo Point
15 km stretch from town. Calm lake, rolling hills, a few boats. Echo Point is fun — shout your name and hear it come back. 45 mins combined for both spots. Quick but satisfying.
🌊 Lake Views📢 Echo Point
12:00 PM
Munnar Town — Lunch + Tea Museum
Kerala rice meals at a local restaurant (₹130–160). KDHP Tea Museum ₹75/person — 45 mins, teaches you about tea processing. Buy loose Munnar tea to take home (₹80–150 for 250g).
🍳 Lunch🥥 Tea Museum ₹75
1:30 PM
Drive: Munnar → Kollam
~210 km via Kothamangalam → Ernakulam Bypass → NH 66 → Kollam. ~4 hrs including a quick tea break. Transition from hill country to coast. Car playlist moment.
🛣 ~4 hrs driveHills → Coast
5:00 PM
Kappil Beach Road — Sunset Drive 🌅
The trip highlight moment. A narrow strip of land with the Arabian Sea on the left and Ashtamudi Lake backwaters on the right. Walk the road, stand between two bodies of water. Insane sunset. Pull the cars over and just be there.
📷 Best Photo Stop🌅 Sunset Point
6:00 PM
Ashtamudi Lake Backwaters — Quick Stop
Park roadside and walk to the lake. Houseboat silhouettes, calm water, Kerala evening light. 20 minutes max — just soak it in before pushing to Varkala.
🌿 Backwaters
8:00 PM
Arrive Varkala — Cliff Check-In + First Night
Check in, freshen up. Head straight to Varkala Cliff for the first night. Grab dinner at a cliff café (₹200–300/person). Walk the cliff promenade. Sea sound below, music, fairy lights above. Welcome to Varkala.
🍽 Cliff Dinner🌒 Cliff Night
Day 3 Full Day Varkala + Return Night May 3 · Saturday
6:00 AM
Sunrise — Varkala Beach Walk
Walk down the cliff steps to the beach. Minimal crowd, cool breeze, golden light across the waves. Group walk on wet sand. The kind of moment you remember 10 years later.
🌅 Sunrise🌊 Beach
8:00 AM
Cliff Breakfast + Cafe Hopping
Try Abba Café, Café del Mar, or The Black Sand Coffee. Full Kerala breakfast or eggs + toast. Sit on cliff-edge seats with sea view. Take your time — no rush today. Budget ₹180–250/person.
☕ Cliff Café🖼 Views
10:00 AM
Bike Rental + Kappil Beach Ride
Rent 5 bikes (₹300–400/day each). Ride 6 km to Kappil Beach — the narrow backwater bridge strip. Swim, sit, hang. No crowd. Black sand, calm backwater, coconut trees. Most chill spot of the trip.
🚴 Bike Ride🏖 Kappil Beach
12:30 PM
Anjengo Fort
18 km from Varkala (30 min ride). British colonial fort with lighthouse and unobstructed ocean views. Very few tourists. Walk the fort walls, see the old cemetery, lighthouse views. 45–60 mins. Worth it.
🏰 Fort📷 Lighthouse Views
2:30 PM
Odayam Black Sand Beach — Photoshoot
Rare black volcanic sand 5 min from main beach. Unique backdrop for photos. Quiet, no hawkers. Creative shots possible — silhouettes, close-up sand, wide ocean. Unusual and different from regular beach photos.
📷 Black SandUnique Spot
4:00 PM
Varkala Beach — Swim + Water Activities
Back to main beach for swimming. Note: strong undercurrents near cliff — swim only in marked zones. Activity options: Parasailing ₹800–1,200 · Surfing lessons ₹600–900 · Kayaking ₹400. Pick one per person based on budget.
🏄 Surf/Para🌊 Swimming
6:00 PM
Cliff Sunset + Shopping Walk 🌅
Best sunset view from the cliff edge. The sea turns gold then crimson. After sunset, browse the cliff shops — jewelry, beach shorts, Kashmiri crafts. Start at 40% of asking price when bargaining. Don't overspend.
🌅 Best Sunset🛍 Shopping
8:00 PM
Seafood Dinner — The Main Event 🦀
Order Kerala fish curry, grilled grouper, tiger prawns, crab masala. Budget ₹250–350/person at mid-range cliff spots. Pro tip: Order Kerala fish thali for best value (₹200–250). Eat slow. Enjoy the sound of waves below.
🦒 Seafood🌒 Cliff Vibes
10:00 PM
Group Rooftop Hangout — Cards + Music
Back at stay. Rooftop session with Bluetooth speaker, card games, trip recap. The moment you'll all talk about for years. Keep it till midnight then pack up bags for return.
🎴 Cards Night🎵 Music
1:00 AM
Return Drive — Varkala to Bangalore
~680 km via Thiruvananthapuram → Nagercoil → NH 44 → Bangalore. 10–11 hours. Stop at Madurai (~5 AM) for chai + parota breakfast. Reach Bangalore by 11 AM–12 PM on May 4.
🚗 Return Night Drive~10 hrs

Tips + Safety

Read these before you leave. Especially the ghat and beach ones.

⛰
Ghat Roads — Munnar
Drive in 2nd gear on steep descents — engine braking, no constant braking
Honk at every blind hairpin bend before you turn
Never halt mid-road for photos — pull over at a viewpoint
Fog is heavy in the morning — slow down, keep headlights on
Keep 15m gap between both cars on ghat bends
🌊
Beach Safety — Varkala
Varkala cliff base has strong undercurrents — do not swim there
Swim only in the marked lifeguard zones near the beach
No swimming after sunset — zero visibility and rising current
Lock valuables in car or locker before entering the sea
If caught in a rip current — swim parallel to shore, not against it
🌒
Night Driving
Rotate drivers strictly every 2–2.5 hours
No highway driving above 80 kmph at night
Keep both cars in convoy — never let one car go too far ahead
Set WhatsApp group for real-time car coordination
Stop every 2 hours — even if driver says they're fine
💰
Budget Control
Collect all money before departure — don't chase mid-trip
Avoid mocktails and desserts at cliff cafes — adds up fast
Barter at cliff shops — start at 40% of asking price
Eat Kerala thalis for lunch — best value per rupee
One person manages daily cash — avoids confusion
👥
Group of 10 Tips
Designate 1 captain per car — final call in any dispute
Set hard departure times — 10 people ready takes 25 mins longer than planned
Pre-assign who sits where — avoid the morning chaos
Bathroom rotation roster at homestays — 10 people 1 bathroom is chaos without it
Shared photo album (Google Photos) — everyone adds from day one
🚗
Varkala Parking
Cliff area has very limited parking — arrive before 6 PM or park near helipad
Paid parking ₹50–100/day near cliff staircase entrance
Better option: Leave cars at stay and rent bikes for local movement
Autos from helipad to cliff cost ₹50–80 for a group
Group Expense Plan

10 people, money chaos avoided with one simple system.

Before Leaving Bangalore
→Collect ₹5,500 from each person. Keep ₹500/person in emergency buffer = ₹5,000 common pool.
→One Finance Head holds the pool cash + makes big payments (fuel, stay).
→Create Splitwise group "Kerala May 26" — add all 10 members.

Fuel Split (Fair Method)
→Keep ALL fuel receipts for both cars throughout the trip.
→At end of trip, add all fuel bills from both cars together.
→Divide total equally by 10. Everyone pays same amount.
→This kills "I sat in car 2 more" arguments completely.
